Introduction

This document specifies the format of font files, and the associated control files, used by the FIGlet and FIGlet4Win programs (FIGdrivers). It is written for designers who wish to build fonts (FIGfonts) usable by either program, and also serves as a standard for development of future versions or similar FIGdrivers. Some features explained here are not supported by both programs. See separate documentation to learn how to use FIGlet or FIGlet4Win.

If you design a FIGfont, please send an e-mail announcement to <figletfonts@figlet.org>, the FIGlet fonts mailing list, and e-mail a copy to <info@figlet.org> for us to put it on the ftp site (ftp://ftp.figlet.org/).
